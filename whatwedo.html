INITIATIVES

The Bird Advocacy Foundation is committed to protecting birds and fostering cooperative relationships between humans and avian wildlife. Their initiatives span across Australia and New Zealand. Additionally, the foundation contributes to bird conservation through initiatives such as creating national parks and reserves to protect critical bird habitats, implementing programs to control introduced predators, supporting community-based conservation efforts focused on habitat restoration education. Their work aims to promote harmonious coexistence between humans our feathered friends.
