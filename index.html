OUR BACKGROUND

The Bird Advocacy Foundation is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to protecting birds and fostering cooperative relationships between humans and avian wildlife. Operating across Australia and New Zealand, their mission is to create harmony between humans and bird wildlife. They acknowledge and respect the Traditional Owners of the land in Australia, including past, present, and emerging custodians. The foundation conducts projects related to education, advocacy, and research, including the Little Chicks Education Program.

WHAT WE HAVE DONE SO FAR

1.)AFRRls (Alternate Feeding Roosting Recreation Location System): These volunteer programs establish safe spaces for birds to feed and rest in special bird sanctuaries.
2.)Education and Advocacy: The foundation actively engages in educational projects, advocating for bird welfare, and conducting research.
3.)Little Chicks Education Program: This initiative empowers school children by educating them about habitat loss, climate change, and the impact on Australian native bird wildlife.
4.)Collaboration with Rural City of Murray Bridge: In early 2023, they collaborated with the Rural City of Murray Bridge to implement an innovative and wildlife-friendly approach to managing Little Corellas in the region.
5.)Championing Bird and Bat Welfare: The foundation works tirelessly to support native wild birds and bats that have been lost, mistreated or are without owners.
6.)Promoting Ethical Wildlife Control: They expose unethical practices within the horticultural public/private industry sectors that engage in culling poisoning or other non-human methods of wildlife control.
